Tufts Emerging Circuits and Systems Lab

Welcome to the TECS Lab. Our research interests are broadly exploring the design of heterogeneous low-power hardware leveraging both CMOS and post-CMOS technologies. Our current focus in on evaluating and designing embedded architectures that leverage dense, fault-prone emerging non-volatile memory technologies across different application scenarios, including energy-efficient, reliable circuits and systems for machine learning.

If you are interested and would like to learn more about research opportunities in our group, please email Prof. Marco Donato.
