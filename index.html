Tufts Emerging Circuits and Systems Lab

Welcome to the TECS Lab! We are broadly interested in the design of heterogeneous low-power hardware leveraging both CMOS and post-CMOS technologies. Our team works at the intersection of VLSI circuits and computer architecture, with the goal of designing energy-efficient, reliable hardware for emerging computing paradigms. Our research focuses on developing cross-stack methodologies that target co-design optimizations at the intersection of devices, circuits, architectures and applications.

If you are interested and would like to learn more about research opportunities in our group, please email Prof. Marco Donato.
